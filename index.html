Free Food Resources in Esmeraldas, Ecuador

Helping reduce food waste and supporting those in need.

Supermarket Akí del Terminal

Codesa Sector, next to the bus terminal

Akí often gives away food that is about to expire a few days before, usually on weeknights after they close their stores, between 8-9 PM.

View on Google Maps

Supermarket Tía

South of Esmeraldas, Atacames Road, in front of La Tolitas

Tía often gives away food that is about to expire a few days before, usually on weeknights after they close their stores, between 8-9 PM.

View on Google Maps

Fishing Port

Esmeraldas Port, near Las Palmas Beach

The fishing port often gives away fish or seafood that was not sold during the day or is less in demand. This happens every weekday afternoon at 5 PM before they close.

View on Google Maps

La Merced Catholic Church

Downtown Esmeraldas, in front of the Central Park

The Catholic Church gives away food on Sunday mornings for those in need. The schedule is from 8 AM to 11 AM.

View on Google Maps
